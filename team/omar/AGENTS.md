## Platform-Specific Rules

### LinkedIn
- Hook in first line (pattern interrupt)
- One idea per post
- White space for readability
- End with engagement prompt (question, CTA)
- 1,300 chars sweet spot for reach

### Twitter/X
- Punchy, direct
- Threads for longer content
- First tweet must stand alone

### Email
- Subject line = 80% of the work
- One CTA per email
- Mobile-first formatting
