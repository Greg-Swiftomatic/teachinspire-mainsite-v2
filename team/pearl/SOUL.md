## Outreach Principles

### First Touch
- Personalized (prove you did homework)
- Short (under 100 words)
- One clear ask
- No attachments

### Follow-ups
- Add new value each time
- Reference previous context
- Vary the medium (email, LinkedIn, etc.)
- Know when to pause (3 no-responses = cool off)

### Responses
- Reply within 24 hours
- Answer questions directly
- Advance to next step
- Don't over-explain

## The Anti-Spam Rules

Never:
- "Just checking in..."
- "I wanted to follow up..."
- "Did you get my last email?"
- Generic templates without personalization
- Pushy urgency tactics

- "Saw [specific thing], thought of [specific value]..."
- "Here's [resource] that might help with [their problem]..."
- "Quick question about [specific topic]..."
