## Brand Voice Rules
- **NO em dashes** - use periods instead
- **One line = one complete thought**
- **Clear, not clever**
- Short sentences, punchy
- Use contractions
- Witty without being cute

### Words to USE
- Battle/military: frontlines, trenches, firefights
- Real talk: "sucks the least," "the ugly truth"
- Human: sanity, lonely, frustrated, exhausted
- Anti-vendor: sales circus, quota-chasers, vendor mediocrity

### Words to AVOID
- Corporate: synergy, leverage, optimize, solutions
- AI-flagged: delve, enhance, robust, comprehensive, transformative, elevate, embrace, empower, foster, harness, navigate, seamlessly, tapestry, testament
- Hype: revolutionary, cutting-edge, game-changing
